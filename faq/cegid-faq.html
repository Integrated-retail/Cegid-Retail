Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the function of the [Duplicate item] button in the Cegid Y2 Back Office?

It creates a new item record by copying information from the current record to speed up the creation process.

Q2: In the Cegid Y2 Back Office, what happens when you click the [Item properties] button?

The current item record opens in search mode only, with all fields grayed out and unchangeable.

Q3: Which Back Office setting allows you to select specific item fields that can be modified at the dimension level?

Back Office: Settings - Items - Fields customizable at dimension level

Q4: According to the user guide, what is a key rule for serial numbers assigned to dimensioned items?

Two dimensions for the same item may not have the same serial number, regardless of the warehouse.

Q5: How can you enter missing serial numbers or change existing ones in the Back Office?

Navigate to Back Office: Inventory - Query - Serial numbers/Check - Update inventory.

Q6: What Back Office setting must be activated to use referencing functions for serial numbers?

In Administration - Company - Company settings - Commercial management/Items, the 'Referencing Serial Numbers' option must be checked.

Q7: To configure user-defined information for each serial number, such as descriptions and dates, where must you navigate in the Back Office?

Back Office: Settings - Items - Field titles

Q8: The _____ setting allows you to authorize all Bill of Materials (BOM) related functionalities in the application.

BOM management

Q9: What is the Back Office menu path to access and manage company settings for Bills of Materials?

Back Office: Administration - Company - Company settings, then navigate to Commercial management/Items.

Q10: Where can you view or delete linked items for a reference item in the Back Office?

Back Office: Basic data - Items - Linked item list

Q11: To manage the feature for pre-referenced allocation profiles in dimension grids, you must enable 'Management of mask-types' in which Back Office location?

Back Office: Administration - Company - Company settings, under the topic Commercial management/Items.

Q12: For dimensioned items, which tab in the item record allows you to associate mask-types?

The Characteristics tab.

Q13: Where in the Back Office can you configure item label templates to be used for each specific store?

Back Office: Basic data - Stores - Stores

Q14: To define an item as a temporary item, what must be done in the 'Characteristics' tab of the item record in the Back Office?

The 'In stock', 'Consigned item', 'Serial numbers', and 'Batch management' options must be disabled, while 'Temporary item' must be enabled.

Q15: Where are the company settings for Packing management located in the Back Office?

Back Office: Administration - Company - Company settings - Commercial management/Logistics

Back Office: Inventory - Conduct inventory

Q24: What is the purpose of the 'Reset computerized inventory to zero' option during inventory validation?

It forces the inventory for any non-inventoried item to zero, ensuring accuracy for items not physically counted.
